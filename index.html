Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy applies to the LangRush mobile application (hereby referred to as the “Application”) developed by Talha Hussain (hereby referred to as the “Service Provider”) as an ad-supported service. This service is provided “AS IS”.

Information Collection and Use

The Application does not require users to create an account and does not collect personally identifiable information such as name, email address, or phone number.

The Service Provider does not collect or store user data on any external server.

However, third-party services used by the Application (such as advertising, AI services, and analytics) may collect certain information automatically, including:

  • Device Internet Protocol (IP) address
  • Device type and operating system
  • App usage statistics
  • Advertising identifiers

This information is used for providing advertisements, improving app performance, and delivering requested services in accordance with third-party privacy policies.

User-Provided Data

The Application allows users to enter text for translation, quiz generation, and summary generation.

User-entered text is processed using third-party artificial intelligence services (OpenAI APIs) to generate translations, quizzes, and summaries.

The Service Provider does not store this text on any external server.

Translation history and favorite words are stored locally on the user’s device only and are not accessible to the Service Provider.

Use of Artificial Intelligence

The Application uses Artificial Intelligence (AI) technologies provided by OpenAI to process user requests.

User-entered text may be transmitted to OpenAI’s servers for processing in accordance with their Privacy Policy:

https://openai.com/policies/privacy-policy

Third-Party Services

The Application uses the following third-party services:

  • Google AdMob (for advertisements)
  • OpenAI APIs (for AI-based processing)
  • Firebase Analytics (for app usage tracking and performance insights)

These services may collect and process data in accordance with their own privacy policies:

  • Google Privacy Policy
  • OpenAI Privacy Policy
  • Firebase Analytics Privacy Policy

The Application also uses Firebase Analytics to collect anonymous usage data, such as screen views, feature usage, and event tracking. This information is used to improve app performance and user experience. No personally identifiable information is collected by Firebase Analytics.

Data Storage

The Application does not use any backend server.

All translation history and favorite words are stored locally on the user’s device.

Users can delete all stored data by clearing the app’s storage or uninstalling the Application.

Children’s Privacy

This Application is intended for users aged 18 years and above.

The Service Provider does not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 13.

If you are a parent or guardian and you believe that your child has provided personal information through the Application, please contact the Service Provider so that the information can be removed.

Security

The Service Provider is committed to protecting user information.

Because no personal data is stored on external servers by the Service Provider, risks related to data breaches are minimized. Local data is protected by the security features of the user’s device.
